Information Sheet for New Employees — Security USA

Information Sheet for New Employees

Welcome to Security USA. We're excited to have you join our team. Please find this list as a helpful resource to get you started.

Payroll Process

  • Payday is every Friday. Each payroll period is Monday 12am to Sunday 11:59pm. You will be paid on the Friday of the following week.
  • ✯ Payroll questions: Payroll@securityusainc.com
  • Check pickup at the NYC office: Mon–Thu 9am–5pm, Fri 7:30am–6pm.
  • Direct deposit: email a voided check or direct deposit form to Payroll@securityusainc.com. Takes 3 weeks to process.
  • Mailed checks go out on Fridays; USPS delivery typically 1–2 business days.

Time and Attendance

  • ✯ Clock in/out daily via the Ehub app or registered site phone. Issues? Call Command Center: 212-594-4475. Failure to clock in/out may result in disciplinary action or payroll delays.
  • Clock in at your scheduled start time and clock out promptly at shift end. You may also call 833-343-1773 from the registered site phone.

Shift End — Wait for Relief

  • ✯ Remain at your post until properly relieved. If relief is more than 15 minutes late, contact the office immediately. Do not leave your site without being officially relieved.

Lateness

  • Be at your post in full uniform at your scheduled start time. No lateness or grace periods. If you anticipate being late, contact the Command Center immediately.

Call Out

  • ✯ Call out at least 4 hours before your shift. Acceptable reasons: illness, doctor's appointment, family emergency, or bereavement. All callouts must be made by calling the Command Center — texts and emails are not permitted.

Uniform / Personal Hygiene

  • ✯ Full uniform required at all times — clean, pressed, and presentable. Proper footwear must be worn on duty. Personal hygiene must be maintained. Uniform inquiries: help@securityusa.info

Drug Policy

  • ✯ Use of illegal drugs or alcohol while working or reporting to work impaired is strictly prohibited. Violations are considered serious misconduct and may result in termination.

Field Inspection

  • ✯ Routine inspections by Field Supervisors or Account Managers may include: a photo of you in proper uniform, review of the command logbook, and a brief electronic knowledge exam.
Accessing Your Payroll — iSolved

Your employee email is used to set up your payroll account in iSolved for paystubs and W-2s. Watch for an activation email from Accudata@myisolved.com.

Questions: Help@securityusainc.com | 24/7 support: 212-594-4475

Sick Leave

  • Earn 1 hour of paid sick leave for every 30 hours worked. Notify the office at least 4 hours before your shift if sick. To claim: email Payroll@securityusainc.com.

Time Off Requests

  • Submit requests via the Ehub app or in person at the office. You must wait for approval.
  • 2 days or less: request at least 14 days in advance. 3+ days: request at least 30 days in advance.

Licensing / Registration

  • Begin renewal at least 3 months before expiration. Once renewed, provide a copy to help@securityusa.info. Assistance: help@securityusainc.com

Minor and Major Violations

  • Read and understand all violations included in your onboarding documents and handbook. Any violation may result in discipline or termination.

Scheduling

  • Your most updated schedule is always on the Ehub app. Questions: call 212-594-4475 or email help@securityusa.info

Flex Guard

  • If you pick up a Flex Guard shift on Ehub, report to the NY office and you'll be assigned to a site anywhere in the 5 boroughs. Questions: Command Center 212-594-4475.
